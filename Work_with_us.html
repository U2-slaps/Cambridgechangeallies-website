About Us

Welcome to Cambridge Charity Allies, a free consultancy dedicated to supporting organisations in the social sector. Our team comprises talented students and graduates from Cambridge and Oxford universities, passionate about creating meaningful change. We offer our expertise to charities, foundations, philanthropists, impact investors, and social enterprises to help them maximise their social impact. At Cambridge Charity Allies, we are committed to working alongside you to address pressing global challenges, whether they be social, economic, environmental, or health-related. We bring a fresh perspective, backed by academic excellence and a genuine passion for positive change.

Our services

Research & Insights

With access to the world-class academic resources and networks at Cambridge and Oxford, we can assist you in identifying research and experts on critical issues affecting your organisation. Whether it's social, economic, environmental, or health-related, we can provide in-depth research, gather relevant data, and write comprehensive reports on topics that matter to you. Our research support can guide your strategic decision-making and help you stay informed on the latest developments in your sector.

Social Media & Digital Presence

As digital natives, we understand the importance of a strong online presence. We can help you build and maintain your social media platforms by creating customised social media strategies tailored to your mission. Whether you need a LinkedIn hiring campaign, impactful content for YouTube, or guidance on running your Instagram or Twitter accounts, we’ve got you covered. We can also assist with developing video content, managing posts, and engaging with your audience to increase your reach and visibility.

Fundraising & Grant Writing

We can help you navigate the world of fundraising and grants. Our team can support you by identifying potential funding opportunities, drafting compelling grant proposals, and developing crowdfunding campaigns. We’ll assist you in articulating your organisation’s vision and impact to attract investors, philanthropists, and donors.

Branding & Communications

Effective communication is key to amplifying your message. We can work with you to craft compelling narratives and impactful messaging to strengthen your brand. Whether through content creation, blogs, newsletters, or marketing materials, we’ll ensure your story resonates with your audience. From creating compelling pitches for donors to refining your organisation’s voice, we’ll help you communicate with clarity and conviction.

Impact Measurement & Reporting

Measuring and reporting your organisation’s impact is essential for long-term success. We assist in creating metrics and performance indicators that allow you to track progress and demonstrate results to your stakeholders. We also provide support in compiling impact reports, which can help you attract more funding, partnerships, and volunteers.

Workshops & Training

We are happy to offer workshops and training sessions tailored to your team’s needs, perhaps an introduction to digital marketing, or social media strategy. If you simply need a brainstorming session with new eyes, we are here for you also. Our goal is to empower your team with the extra skills and knowledge needed to advance your mission further.

Creative Design & Website Development

In today’s digital world, a well-designed website and visually compelling materials are essential for telling your story and engaging your audience. Our team can help you build or redesign your website to ensure it is user-friendly, visually appealing, and effectively communicates your mission. In addition to web development, we can create beautifully designed materials to suit your needs, such as brochures, infographics, reports, and presentations.
